## ✨ Key Features

### Interactive Self-Check Widget
```
User sees 4 reflective questions
     ↓
Checks box 1: "I process information differently" ☑
Checks box 2: "I have stable patterns" ☑
Checks box 3: "These are part of my identity" ☑
     ↓
[SUCCESS MESSAGE APPEARS WITH FADE-IN ANIMATION]
✨ "Your self-reflection has given you clarity.
   The tests below can help you understand more deeply."
     ↓
User feels: Validated, competent, ready to explore
```

### Empowerment Cards

**Card 1: What is neurodivergence?**
- Neurobiological variation (not disorder)
- People can describe their own experiences
- Valid without expert confirmation

**Card 2: Why self-diagnosis matters**
- Many self-identify BEFORE professional diagnosis
- Self-understanding → first step to self-acceptance
- Your perspective is legitimate without validation

## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Selbstwirksamkeit (Self-Efficacy)
Users feel competent to understand themselves without depending on external authorities.

### Neurodiversity
Not a disorder. Just a different way of processing information.

### Self-Diagnosis
Valid and often the FIRST step. Professional diagnosis is optional.

### User Autonomy
Users control their exploration at their own pace without judgment.
